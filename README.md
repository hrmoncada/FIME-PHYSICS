* *Physics 2:*  The course of Physics II belongs to the General Studies area. It is theoretical–practical–experimental in nature and is a mandatory course. Its purpose is to enable students to develop competencies in critical thinking, teamwork, and research. The content is organized into units:
     * Unit I: Elasticity and Deformation;
     * Unit II: Oscillations and Waves;
     * Unit III: Fluid Mechanics;
     * Unit IV: Heat, Temperature, and the Laws of Thermodynamics.
